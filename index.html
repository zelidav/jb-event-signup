Three tiers, all consignment. You pay only for sellable inventory — display case, art glass, and steeze pieces are JBD-funded display only.

Two-mode program. Wholesale items (Clearboys, Box Sets, Hats, Papers, Pre-Rolls, Flower) ship at 50% of MSRP — you pay upfront and keep 100% of MSRP at the till. Consignment items (case build, art glass, steeze, display jars) ship at $0 upfront — if a customer buys one, you keep 20% of MSRP and remit 80% back to JBD. Display still doubles as branded merchandising — the consignment GP is upside, not the core economics.
